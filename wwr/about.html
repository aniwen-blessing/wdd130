River Rush Rafting

Our mission is to provide safe, thrilling, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences while preserving the natural beauty of our rivers.

History

Founded in 2010 by veteran river guide Jake Thompson, River Rush Rafting began as a small local operation and has grown into one of the most trusted names in adventure tourism across the Pacific Northwest.
